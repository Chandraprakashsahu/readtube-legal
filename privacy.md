# Privacy Policy for ReadTube

**Last updated:** Febriry 14, 2026

**ReadTube** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide you with the best experience:

*   **Personal Information:** When you sign up, we may collect your email address and name (via Google Sign-In or email registration).
*   **Usage Data:** We collect anonymous data about how you use the app (e.g., number of summaries generated) to improve our AI services.
*   **Device Information:** We may collect information about your mobile device (e.g., device model, operating system) for crash reporting and analytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
*   To create and manage your account.
*   To generate AI summaries from YouTube videos.
*   To sync your saved summaries across devices.
*   To provide customer support.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   We use **Supabase** (a secure cloud database) to store your account information.
*   All data transmission is encrypted using SSL (HTTPS) technology.
*   We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. AI & Content Generation

ReadTube uses Artificial Intelligence to generate text and audio summaries.
*   We do not host original YouTube videos.
*   Summaries are generated for educational purposes.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
*   **Google Play Services**
*   **Expo** (for app updates)
*   **Supabase** (Authentication & Database)

## 6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your data at any time.
*   **To delete your account:** Go to App Settings > Account > Delete Account.
*   Your data will be permanently removed from our servers immediately.
